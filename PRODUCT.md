# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

People who want a **mental map of Scripture** — where books and chapters live relative to each other — and practice that familiarity in spare minutes, mostly on a phone. Primary: solo daily habit. Secondary: endless practice rounds for reps when the daily is done.

## Product Purpose

**Versemark** is a "place the marker" Scripture familiarity game. A BSB verse appears; the player drops a pin on a continuous 1,189-chapter canon timeline and confirms. Score rewards both precision (chapter distance) and independence (how few hints). Success is not trivia recall: it is growing a spatial sense of the canon so passages feel locatable, not random.

## Positioning

**Familiarity over flash** — the map of the canon is the product; every round trains the same continuous axis until Scripture feels ordered under the player's hand.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, literary, focused. A calm daily ritual, not a spectacle. Warm without being precious, serious without being solemn. The feeling of a well-worn reading desk: paper, ink, a clean margin, a marker laid down.

## Anti-references

- Garish oversaturated game chrome that trivializes the text.
- Gamification kitsch (coins, streak-on-fire emoji, cartoon trophies, confetti).
- Flat "Bible app" pastel minimalism and generic SaaS gradients.
- Multiple-choice bookshelf or drill-down grids that hide global proportion.
- AI-default cream/sand/parchment backgrounds with no real color identity.

## Design Principles

1. **The canon is the board** — the full-width timeline teaches proportion and order; chrome never competes with it.
2. **Calm over juice** — motion is minimal; a correct guess is a quiet mark landing, not a celebration.
3. **One thumb, five minutes** — portrait touch play is the origin; every interaction is reachable one-handed in under a second.
4. **Warmth through type and accent** — warmth lives in the serif typography and a single terracotta accent, not in a tinted background.
5. **Backend-free honesty** — same daily for everyone from a date seed; state lives with the player.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on all text. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` (no ambient loops, minimal transitions). Touch targets ≥ 44px; primary actions reachable one-handed in the bottom third. Avoid encoding critical state only in color (hint multiplier and score also use text).
